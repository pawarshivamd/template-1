Fiskardo

Fiskardo company for steel trading in Iraq, established in Erbil since 2023 holding a long business experience of steel & Iron trading over than 12 years in Middle east, the main object of Fiskardo is to purchase of steel from China that was manufactured in China and sell these materials to the local steel market in Iraq

Our Mission

Our Future planning is to establishing a Steel pipes manufacture for Hot Rolled steel and Galvanized steel pipes,

We interested to dealing with deferent kind of steel that used in building and construction industries like Pipe manufactories that we supports with row materials to introduce the steel Tube and pipes,

Also the other kinds of materials like Pre-Painted galvanized steel (PPGI) used for manufactures of Air Conditions, Refrigerators and other home applications
